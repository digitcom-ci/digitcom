Gilsonite

Gilsonite is a natural solid hydrocarbon known for its unique properties and great versatility. Used in many industrial sectors, it plays an essential role in the formulation of drilling fluids, the improvement of the performance of bituminous asphalt and the manufacture of high-quality inks. Thanks to its particular chemical structure, it offers excellent strength, superior adhesion and increased stability in various applications. Aware of its importance for many areas, we ensure a constant supply of top quality gilsonite, meeting the specific requirements of each industry.

Fuel Oil

Fuel oil is an essential petroleum product, widely used as an energy source in industries, electricity generation and shipping. Thanks to its high energy density, it allows efficient combustion and a stable supply for large energy infrastructures and marine engines. Adapted to the requirements of companies seeking to optimize their energy consumption, our fuel oil is rigorously controlled to guarantee optimal performance and a controlled environmental footprint. We are committed to providing reliable, high-quality fuel, meeting the specific needs of our customers in various industries.

Base Oil

Base oil is the fundamental element in the manufacture of lubricants and greases used in the automotive, industrial and marine sectors. Thanks to its viscosity and thermal stability properties, it ensures efficient lubrication, reducing the wear of mechanical components and prolonging the life of equipment. Our range includes premium base oils, specially formulated to offer superior performance and meet the requirements of the most demanding environments. By providing high-quality products, we help to improve the efficiency and reliability of machines and motors used in various applications.

Solar Energy

Solar energy represents a sustainable and innovative alternative to traditional energy sources. Operated through photovoltaic panels and thermal systems, it makes it possible to produce clean and renewable electricity, thus reducing dependence on fossil fuels and the carbon footprint. Increasingly adopted by industries, companies and individuals, it offers an effective solution to meet energy needs while contributing to the transition to a greener future. We are committed to promoting the use of solar energy by providing solutions adapted to different sectors, guaranteeing performance, profitability and respect for the environment.
